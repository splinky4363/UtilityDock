Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

UtilityDock ("we", "the site") is a static website. This policy explains what happens to your data when you use it.

No accounts, no server-side processing

UtilityDock has no user accounts, databases or backend server. Every calculator, converter, generator and text tool runs entirely as JavaScript inside your own browser. The numbers or text you type into a tool are processed on your device and are never transmitted to us or stored anywhere.

Cookies and analytics

We do not use cookies, analytics scripts or advertising trackers of any kind. Your one exception is your theme preference (light/dark mode), which is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and never leaves your device.

Third-party services

  • Hosting: This site is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log standard technical information (such as IP address and browser type) as part of operating its infrastructure — see GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • QR Code Generator: This one page loads a small open-source JavaScript library from the jsDelivr CDN to draw QR codes. Loading that script may expose your IP address to jsDelivr in the same way any external resource request does; the text you encode into a QR code is never sent to jsDelivr or anywhere else.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no personal data at all, UtilityDock does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the project's GitHub repository.
