About this tool

Use this calculator to add VAT to a net (pre-tax) price, or to reverse-calculate and remove VAT from a gross (tax-included) price — common when pricing products or checking receipts.

The default rate is 20% but you can edit it to match your country's VAT or sales tax rate.

How to use the vat calculator

  1. Enter an amount and the VAT rate (defaults to 20%).
  2. Choose whether the amount already includes VAT or not.
  3. Read the net, VAT and gross figures below.

Frequently asked questions

How do I remove VAT from a total?

Switch to “Amount includes VAT”, enter the total and the rate — the tool divides out the tax to show you the net price and the VAT portion.

How is VAT added to a price?

Net price × (1 + rate/100) = gross price. Switch to “Amount excludes VAT” and enter your net price to see this instantly.

Can I use this for sales tax instead of VAT?

Yes, the maths is identical — just enter your local sales tax rate instead of a VAT rate.
