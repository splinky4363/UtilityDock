About this tool

A live text-analysis tool for writers, students and editors: it counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences and paragraphs, and estimates reading time at an average 200 words per minute.

Everything updates as you type — there's no submit button and no text ever leaves your browser.

How to use the word counter

  1. Paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Watch the counts update live as you type.
  3. Use “Clear” to start over.

Frequently asked questions

How is reading time calculated?

Word count divided by 200 words per minute (a common average adult silent-reading speed), rounded up to the nearest minute.

Does it count words in other languages?

It splits text on whitespace, so it works reasonably well for space-separated languages; character-based languages without spaces will count as fewer “words” than characters.

Is there a word limit?

No — it's limited only by your browser's memory, so it comfortably handles anything from a tweet to a full manuscript.
