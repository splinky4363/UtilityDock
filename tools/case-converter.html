Case Converter

Switch text between upper, lower, title and sentence case.

About this tool

Quickly reformat text for headings, titles or body copy without retyping it. Choose UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case or Sentence case and the transformation is applied instantly to the text box below.

How to use the case converter

  1. Paste your text into the box.
  2. Click the case style you want applied.
  3. Copy the converted text with one click.

Frequently asked questions

What does Title Case do?

It capitalises the first letter of every word — useful for headlines and titles.

What does Sentence case do?

It capitalises only the first letter of each sentence and lower-cases the rest, like standard prose.

Will it change my line breaks or spacing?

No, only letter casing changes — your original line breaks and spacing are preserved.
