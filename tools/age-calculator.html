About this tool

This tool calculates precise age in years, months and days between two dates, handling leap years and varying month lengths correctly — no rough estimates.

It also tells you how many total days you've been alive and how many days remain until your next birthday.

How to use the age calculator

  1. Enter your date of birth.
  2. Optionally change the “as of” date — it defaults to today.
  3. See your exact age plus days until your next birthday.

Frequently asked questions

Does it account for leap years?

Yes. The calculation walks calendar months and days directly, so February 29th and varying month lengths are handled correctly.

Can I calculate age as of a future or past date?

Yes — change the “as of” date to any date to see age at that point in time, useful for eligibility or legal calculations.

Is my birth date stored anywhere?

No. The calculation runs entirely in your browser and nothing is sent to a server or saved.
